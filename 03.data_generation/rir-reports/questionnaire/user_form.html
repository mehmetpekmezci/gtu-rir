Table 6.1: RIR Human Evaluation Questionnaire

To evaluate the RIRs generated by 7 different neural network models, we selected random audio samples from the GTU-RIR dataset, which was recorded in 11 different rooms within the GTU Computer Engineering Department.

For each room, we provided the participants with the real song recorded using the selected microphone in that room. In addition to this, we provided the same song reverberated using both the real RIR and synthetic RIRs generated by the models.

We ask to rate the similarity between the real recorded song and the artificially reverberated versions (using both real and synthetic RIRs) on a scale from 1 to 10, where a score of 1 indicates the least similarity and a score of 10 indicates the highest similarity :
